# Street-warrior
- An endless runner game.
- The platforms and tile map are pulled to the left direction, with a slight speed difference.
- The score tracer is in continues increase, unless the player falled or eliminated.
- Each coin is collected, increases the score by 20 points.
- If the player fall from the platform or go with it and exceed the left limit of the screen, he would die.
- The current score restart each time the player die, while the best score save the highest score that the player reached.
- The best score keep saving the score, unless the user close the game.
